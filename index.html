Why two feeds

Two legislatures, side by side

Followed together, the feeds do something neither does alone: they let you watch the world's two largest democratic legislative systems work in parallel, in real time. The same policy problem — AI, climate, trade — often moves through Congress and through the Parliament and Council at the same moment, by strikingly different routes. There is no better standing illustration of how the two systems differ.

The method

Built to be checkable

Every claim in every post traces to an official source — Congress.gov for the US feed; the European Parliament's open-data service, the EU Publications Office and the OEIL legislative observatory for the EU feed. Anyone can pull the record and check a post. We think they should.

Official sources only Posts are generated from government legislative databases — never from news coverage.
Complete, not curated The routine steps get posted too. Completeness is what makes a record trustworthy.
AI, labeled Plain-English summaries are AI-generated and carry an [AI-Generated] label. Facts, dates and vote numbers come from the record verbatim.
Corrected in the open Errors are fixed visibly, never silently — every correction is listed in a public log.
