BillTracking — copy-paste syllabus blurbs
=========================================

US feed (American politics / Congress / con-law courses):

  We will follow @USBillTracker on X (x.com/USBillTracker) this semester — an automated,
  source-linked feed that posts every step of legislation moving through Congress, from
  committee to the President's desk, straight from the Congress.gov record. Free and public;
  background at billtracking.org.

EU feed (EU politics / European law / EU-studies courses):

  We will follow @EUBillTracker on X (x.com/EUBillTracker) this semester — an automated,
  plain-English feed of every legislative procedure moving through the European Parliament
  and Council, from the EU's official registers. Free and public; background at
  billtracking.org.

Both feeds (comparative politics / comparative legislatures):

  We will follow @USBillTracker and @EUBillTracker on X this semester — twin automated feeds
  posting every step of US and EU lawmaking from the official records, in one format. Watching
  the two systems process legislation side by side is the course's running case study. Free and
  public; background at billtracking.org.
